Consulting

For Co-operatives: Whether you are an established co-op or are just starting out, I can assist you with any stage of your co-operative development.  I provide high-quality technical assistance, supporting all aspects of co-op development (from member drives to business planning to board training and more!)  Read testimonials from my clients here.  

Empowering others is how I lead.  After five years of rigorous study and practice, I am well-versed in cooperative development "best practices".  I have worked with all types of co-ops and studied in a variety of modalities (anti-oppression, popular education, permaculture, and traditional co-op development). My goal is to help educate, train and guide you to make the best decisions for your co-op's integrity and success!

For Social Enterprises, Non-Profits and Other Groups: My professional skills include facilitating board meetings and community dialogues; training and culture-building; and analyzing, addressing and designing solutions for poorly functioning group processes, dynamics and organizational systems.
