About Caroline/Clara

Hello there.  My name is Caroline.  I am also known as Clara.

I am a multimedia artist and consultant for democratically organized groups (such as cooperatives and collectives). My main creative modes are writing, music and video production (somedays I dance too!) I am shaped by my diverse life experiences and my deep inquiries into narratives, meanings of sustainability, and the teachings + practices of Buddhism.

I practice openness and awareness whenever possible. I consider being in such a mode as akin to highly cooperative and generative state (also called “flow”). I orient to my world with a lens of flows and rhythms of energy. This orientation unifies my diverse works.

Caroline is the legal name and professional appearance. Clara is the uncompromising creative force. Together they cohabit a beloved person living, working and expressing in Denver, Colorado, USA.
