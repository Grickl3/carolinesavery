About Caroline/Clara

Hello there. My name is Caroline. I am also known as Clara. Or Claraline. 

I am a multimedia creative and consultant/coach for democratically organized groups (such as cooperatives and collectives). My main creative modes are writing, music and video production (somedays I dance too!) I am shaped by my diverse life experiences and my deep inquiries into narratives, meanings of sustainability, and the teachings and practices of Buddhism.

I practice openness and awareness as often and as deeply as possible. This looks like abiding in a highly cooperative, integrative and generative state (also called “flow”). I see the world through a lens of as flows and strata, patterns and rhythms of energy. And I tend to engage as a catalyst and vision-holder for beneficial disruptions. This overarching orientation weaves together my diverse body of works.

Thannissaro Bhikkhu in The Karma of Question says the Buddha said that “Who am I?” is an unskillful question, and I would tend to agree. I can share, however, and with some degree of acuity, about the patterns defining my existence--thanks to my lifelong stream-of-consciousness writing practice. To explore of my autobiographical writing, please attend my blog.

Caroline is the legal name and professional appearance. Clara is the uncompromising creative force. Together they cohabit a beloved person living, working and expressing in Denver, Colorado, USA.
