Artist's Bio

As sweet as honey or the taste of steel, All-American like post-industrialist apple pie, Clara Bland deftly bridges the gaps between rock genres like so much black electrical tape.  She’ll blend snarky post-punk, moody goth, richly emotional folk ballads, textured skunky blues… and the outcome is yummy, if undeniably dark.  Fearless traversing sounds, Clara can handle a creepy New-Orleans-style folk ballad just as well as a feedback-laden grunge yowl.  May no genre go untested by Clara, who has been playing guitar for 14 years and singing for longer.  Her ideal band would be a blend of Cub, Sebadoh and Spiritualized, with a sprinkle of the Dandy Warhols baked in.

Clara Bland is the musician persona of Caroline Savery, a multimedia-maker based in Denver, CO, who also moonlights as a filmmaker, writer, and professional democratic organizations designer and consultant.
